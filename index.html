Story

Starfish, also known as sea stars, are marine animals found in oceans all around the world. Despite their name, they are not actually fish. Starfish have five arms and a unique ability to regenerate lost limbs. They move slowly using tube feet and feed mostly on shellfish. The Lone Starfish in this photo rests quietly on a sandy seabed, blending beautifully with its surroundings.
